Sev Puri

Description

This dish is famous in India especially in Maharashtra. This is a chat item filled with all spicy-tangy flavours which will burst in your mouth when you eat a whole puri at once.

Ingredients

  • flat-puri
  • sev
  • 1 finely diced tomato
  • 1 finely diced onion
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • sweet chutney(made with dates and tamarind)
  • spicy chutney(made with green chilly and coriander) Or red chilly powder
  • finely chopped coriander
  • diced lemon
  • chat masala

Steps:

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes. Add chat masala and red chilly powder as per your taste.
  2. Place all the flat puris in a plate.
  3. Put 1/2 spoon mashed potato on each puri and make sure it covers the whole surface of the puri.
  4. Put 1 spoon sweet chutney on all puris
  5. Add diced onions and tomatoes on each puri as much as each puri can fit
  6. Add 1/2 spoon spicy chutney on each puri
  7. Put sev on all puris
  8. Garnish it with coriander and drizzle with sweet chutney and chat masala,red chilly powder and salt.
  9. Enjoy:)

Note: All spices and masalas measures are subjective to one's taste. Add according to your own.
