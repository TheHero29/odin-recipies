Sevaya Kheer

Description

The Marathi word Sevaya means vermicelli in the English language and called Semiya in South India. Kheer is a pudding like dish made with milk but not as thick as the western puddings and has a flowing consistency.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ghee
  • 1 cup sevaya(vermicelli)
  • 1.5 cup milk
  • 4-5 cashew nuts
  • 3 cloves
  • 4-5 raisins
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • sugar as needed

Steps

  1. Melt the ghee in a deep pan on low flame
  2. Add the raisins,cashews and cloves
  3. Add Sevaya(vermicelli) and stir
  4. Pour milk and add sugar as per taste.
  5. bring to boil
  6. Once the sevaya(vermicelli) has softened, add cardamom, stir and serve.
  7. Enjoy:)
