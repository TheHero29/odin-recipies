Pav Bhaji

Description

This is a very popular recipe in India, liked by almost everyone. You may have to go to a nearby Asian Indian store to get the 'Pav Bhaji Masala' that is the special spice to bring the flavor (Masala means spice). Pav is actually the buns. Bhaji is the vegetables cooked with spice.

Ingredients

  • pav(bread)
  • butter
  • 2-3 tomatoes
  • finely chopped onions
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • 1 cup cauli-flower
  • 3-4 capsicums
  • coriander
  • diced lemon
  • 1 cup green peas
  • chopped garlic
  • garam masala

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a wok over medium heat.
  2. Saute garlic and green chile for 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in onions and ginger.
  4. Cook until onions are brown.
  5. Then add tomatoes, and cook until pasty.
  6. Stir in cauliflower, cabbage, peas, carrots and potatoes.
  7. Season with pav bhaji masala.
  8. Cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Butter the pavs(bread) and put them on a pan till they turn golden-red
